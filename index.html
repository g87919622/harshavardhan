army Service

army service

"Army service" can refer to several things, including the Indian Army's Army Service Corps, which handles logistics, or the general concept of military service, which involves a commitment to a nation's army. For those interested in joining, options include roles as soldiers or officers, with different entry points and selection processes such as officer exams (e.g., NDA, CDS) or enlistment after completing a certain level of education

navy service

Navy service involves a career in a naval force, with roles ranging from operating ships and aircraft to specialized positions like marine commando or diver. The specific duties, training, and recruitment process vary depending on the country and the branch (officer or enlisted)

air force service

"Air force service" can refer to military service in an air force, like the Indian Air Force (IAF) or U.S. Air Force, which includes roles in combat, support, and logistics, or it can refer to specific support services provided to airmen, such as food and recreation services. This service is available to both officers and enlisted personnel and can include career paths in the flying branch, ground duty, or technical roles, with options for both permanent and short-service commissions
